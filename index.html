Who am I?

Hi there! I'm HeuiChan (Terrence) Lim, an assistant professor in the Mathematics and Computer Science department at Davidson College. I teach Data Structures, Software Design, and Compilers. My research focuses on compilers (both static and just-in-time), dynamic program analysis, software engineering, and programming languages. I specialize in using dynamic program analysis to identify and address faults in compilers, enhancing the debugging experience for developers and swiftly resolving security concerns. Please feel free to explore my research and publications, and don't hesitate to contact me for collaboration opportunities!
